Land Registry

Smart Property storing property information in a distributed Blockchain network to provide trusted and accurate property data.
A System which underpins the economy by securing the ownership of all the registered land property.

Public(Property Owner)

Interested Party owning a property using land registry Blockchain to search and verify property information for ownership rights documents,
can attach prperty transfer documents and agreement between both the parties while selling a property.

Public(Property Buyer)

Once the agreement is done on the paper for purchasing the property, seller initiates the title transfer function from the portal by attaching the reqired documents.
Buyer will get a notification to approve the title transfer initiative by seller and which again will be confirmed and verified by the Land Registry Department.
